| Jett

\\Biography

Representing her home country of South Korea,
Jett's agile and evasive fighting style lets her
take risks no one else can. She runs circles around
every skirmish, cutting enemies before they even know
what hit them.

Special Abilities 

  | Updraft

 

 

 

INSTANTLY propel Jett high int

   | Cloudburst 

 

 

 

 

INSTANTLY throw a projectile that
expands into a brief vision-blocking
cloud on impact with a surface.
HOLD the ability key to curve the smoke in
the direction of your crosshair.

   | Tailwind

 

 

 

INSTANTLY propel Jett in the
direction she is moving. If
Jett is standing still, she will
propel forward.
