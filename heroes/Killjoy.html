| KillJoy

\\Biography

The genius of Germany. Killjoy secures
the battlefield with ease using her
arsenal of inventions. If the damage
from her gear doesn't stop her enemies,
her robots' debuff will help make short
work of them.

Special Abilities 

 | Alarmbot

 

 

 

EQUIP a covert Alarmbot. FIRE to deploy a bot
that hunts down enemies that get in range.
After reaching its target, the bot explodes,
applying Vulernable. HOLD EQUIP to recall a
deployed bot.

   | NANOSWARM

 

 

 

 

EQUIP a Nanoswarm grenade. FIRE to throw the grenade.
Upon landing, the Nanoswarm goes covert.
ACTIVATE the Nanoswarm to deploy
a damaging swarm of nanobots.

   | Turret

 

 

 

EQUIP a Turret. FIRE to deploy a turret
that fires at enemies in a 180 degree cone.
HOLD EQUIP to recall the deployed turret.

 

   | LOCKDOWN

 

 

EQUIP the Lockdown device. FIRE to
deploy the device. After a long
windup, the device Detains all
enemies caught in the radius. The
device can be destroyed by enemies.
